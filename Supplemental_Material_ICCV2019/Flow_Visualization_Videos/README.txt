This folder contains two subfolders
	(1) MVF_Videos : This folder contains 4 videos, demonstrating the effectiveness of CS, PEOF and CS + PEOF in estimating the MVF. The recovered flows are arranged in form of collages for better comparison. 
	(2) Restored_Videos : This folder contains the videos restored by CS+PEOF for the 'Floor' and 'Fabric' videos. For the restoration results of other two videos, please see 'Restoration_Videos' under main folder.
